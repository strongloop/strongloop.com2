Supporting the open source community

StongLoop began in 2013 offering an open-source and enterprise version of Node.js. Acquired by IBM in 2015, the StrongLoop team continues to build LoopBack, the open-source framework. StrongLoop and IBM continue to contribute and support the Open Source community.

Projects:

  • Microgateway
